About Us

Founded in 2020, Happy Café began as a small coffee cart with a simple dream — to serve fresh, high-quality coffee and create a space where people feel truly welcome. Every cup we brew is made from carefully selected beans, roasted to perfection, and served with a smile. Our cozy, loft-inspired space is designed for everything from friendly catch-ups to quiet moments with a book. At Happy Café, it’s not just about coffee — it’s about community, connection, and creating memories. We look forward to sharing our passion with you, one cup at a time.
